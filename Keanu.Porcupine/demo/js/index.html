Porcupine Demo

This demo allows you to change the color of the smart lamp (above) using Porcupine. Note that you need a working microphone. This demo is running locally in the browser. You can turn off your internet connection and it will keep working.

The wake phrase is Hey Edison. After the wake phrase is detected the lamp turns red and starts listening to follow-on color name. Available colors are Hot Pink , Deep Pink , Fire Brick , Papaya Whip , Peach Puff , Sandy Brown , Lime Green , Forest Green , Deep Sky Blue , Magenta , White Smoke , Lavender Blush , and Dim Gray .
